CPF Explained Simply 🎓

CPF (Central Provident Fund) is Singapore's mandatory savings scheme. Think of it as a forced savings plan for your housing, healthcare, and retirement.

The 3 CPF Accounts:

🏠 Ordinary Account (OA)
Use for HDB flat purchase, monthly mortgage, or CPF Investment Scheme (CPFIS). Earns 2.5% p.a.
⭐ Special Account (SA)
For retirement. Earns 4% p.a. — consider topping this up for tax relief! Up to S$8,000/year contribution gets you tax deduction.
💊 Medisave Account (MA)
For hospitalisation bills, MediShield Life premiums, and approved medical expenses. Also earns 4% p.a.
💡 Fresh Grad Tip: If you have extra cash at end of year, top up your SA (up to S$8,000) to get an income tax deduction AND earn 4% interest. It's one of the best guaranteed returns in Singapore.
